## 🚀 Using the Application

You can open the app using the new desktop shortcut. When the window opens, you see the sign-in screen.

### Creating an Account
You need an account to save your work. 
1. Click the "Sign Up" button on the main screen. 
2. Enter your email address and a strong password. 
3. The system checks your credentials using security tokens to keep your data safe.
4. Verify your email to complete the registration.

### Joining a Room
Collaboration happens within specific rooms. Each room acts as a project board.
1. Log in with your email and password.
2. Click the "Create Room" button to start a new project.
3. Share the room name or code with your team members.
4. Team members enter the same room code to join your session.
5. The empty canvas appears. Everyone who joins sees the same background.

### Working on the Canvas
The main window contains a toolbar with various drawing tools. 
- Pencil: Use this to sketch lines or shapes.
- Shapes: Insert pre-made squares, circles, or triangles to organize your ideas.
- Text: Add labels or notes to specific parts of your canvas.
- Pointer: Use this to move items or resize objects.

Changes you make show up on the screen of every person in the room within a fraction of a second. The app handles the synchronization automatically. You do not need to click a save button. The database stores every stroke and move as you make it.

## 🔒 Staying Secure

Security keeps your project data private. drawpio uses encrypted connections to verify your identity. This prevents unauthorized access to your rooms. Always use a unique password for your account to maintain safety. If you forget your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the sign-in screen to reset your credentials.

## 🔍 Troubleshooting Common Issues

Sometimes software does not behave as expected. Try these steps if you encounter an error.

- App will not open: Close all other programs and try again. If it still fails, restart your computer.
- Cannot see others: Verify your internet connection. A slow connection can delay the live updates. If others are in the room, try leaving the room and joining again.
- Drawing feels slow: Close background apps like web browsers or video players to free up your computer memory.
- Cannot log in: Check for typos in your email address. Ensure your caps lock key is off when you type your password.

## 📈 Improving Your Experience

For the best experience, use a mouse or a drawing tablet. A touchscreen also works well if your monitor supports it. Keeping your internet connection wired instead of using Wi-Fi often leads to faster updates for your canvas tools.
